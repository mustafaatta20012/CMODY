Secret of the Golden Crema

Crema is the rich, creamy amber-colored layer atop an espresso shot. The secret lies in the blend ratio: 70% Arabica + 30% Robusta + 9 Bar Pressure = the thickest crema and most beautiful appearance.
Tip: Use freshly roasted beans (4–14 days old) for optimal results.

Scientifically Proven Benefits

Memory & Concentration

Caffeine blocks adenosine receptors, increasing alertness and improving short-term cognitive memory.

Mood Enhancement

Coffee triggers dopamine and serotonin release. A Harvard study shows 4 cups daily reduces depression by 20%.

Athletic Performance

Caffeine releases free fatty acids into the bloodstream as fuel, improving endurance by 11–12%.

Powerful Antioxidants

Coffee is the single largest dietary source of antioxidants in the Western diet, offering over 1,000 active compounds.

Tips for Safe Enjoyment

Best Time to Drink Coffee

Avoid coffee during the first hour after waking up. Optimal windows are: 9:30–11:30 AM or 1:30–5:00 PM.

Always Stay Hydrated

Coffee has a mild diuretic effect. Drink a glass of water alongside every cup to maintain healthy hydration.

Children & Teens

Avoid caffeine for children under 12 years. For teenagers, the recommended daily cap is 100 mg.
